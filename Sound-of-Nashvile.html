The Sound of Nashville Project Stages 

CONCEPTING

In our team, we tasked with choosing a location, name, theme, rides, costs, special events, concessions, management positions, along with other theme park asspects.

INITIAL FOUNDATION

We were tasked with creating a presentation encompassing all of the parts of the theme park, our mission, vision, and value statements as well as information about the local area and demographics.

VISUAL DESIGN

We were tasked with designing the park's layout, logo, rides, games, menu items, and advertisements along with other aesthetics.

PRESENTATION

Finally, our team had to present our theme park, local demographics, mission, vission, and value statements, our positions, and what separtes us from our competition to a team of investors.
